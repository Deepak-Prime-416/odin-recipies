Hot Chat

Ingredients

  • Chana
  • Potato
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Chat Masala
  • Green Chillies
  • Chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Carrot
  • Lemon
  • Corriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil Chana
  2. Mash Half of the Boiled Chana
  3. Add 2 tbl spoon of oil into kadai and let it heat
  4. Add chopped onions, tomato, salt, chilli powder, chat masala, Mashed Potato and boiled chana in the kada
  5. Add One glass of water into the kadai and Boil the ingredients
  6. Now Add the mashed Chana Paste into the Boiling mixture
  7. Serve them on a Plate with onions and corriander garnishing
