Ramen

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • 4 cloves,Garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
  • 4 ounces shittake Mushrooms
  • Soy sauce
  • Spinach
  • Thinly Sliced Green onions
  • Grated ginger
  • Yaki-soba,seasoning sauce

Instructions

  1. boil eggs in a pan and put those boiled eggs aside.
  2. Heat 2-3 tbl spoons of Olive oil.
  3. Add minced garlic, cloves, grated ginger and stir frequently.
  4. Whisk in chicken broth, mushrooms, soy sauce and 3 cups of water.
  5. Boil all of them untill mushrooms are softened about 10 minutes.
  6. Stir in Yaki Soba until loosened and cooked through about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Stir in spinach. carrot and green onions until spinach begins to wilt, about 2 minutes.
  8. Serve immediately, granished with eggs.
  9. Yummy and Tasty ramen is ready enjoy
