Biriyani

Ingredients

  • Rice/Basmati Rice(Preffered)
  • Chicken Pieces
  • Chilli Powder
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • Mint leaves
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Biriyani Masala
  • Green Chillies
  • Ginger Garlic Paste

Instructions

  1. Wash the Chicken.
  2. Wash the Rice in fresh water and soak them.
  3. Add 1 tbl spoon salt, 2 tbl spoon chilli powder, sufficient Biriyani Masala to the chicken and marinate it for 30 mins.
  4. Take a Bowl enough for Biriyani, add tender cuts of Onions, Green Chillies, Bay leaf, Mint leaves and fry them till they get golden brown colour.
  5. Add the marinated chicken to the fried veggies.
  6. Fry the chicken till it gets 50% cooked.
  7. Add the soaked Rice to the chicken mixture.
  8. Add Twice the water for the Volume of Rice.
  9. Add any flavouring agents if you want to the Water.
  10. Add salt if necessary.
  11. Let the Biriyani Boil untill all the water is drained.
